OUR TEAM

Our team members aren't just here to make a paycheck. Everyone on the team is valued and their passion for their craft is encouraged and mentored. We are experts in our field and do great work because we love what we do and we take pride in what we produce.


Between shoots you can find us in our studio playing very serious games of ping pong!

Mohamed Somji

Studio Assistant & Photographer

Mohamed heads Gulf Photo Plus and started Seeing Things in 2015 as a way to shoot more, but it quickly became a diverse and renowned studio working with the biggest clients in the region. As a photographer, his reportage style lends unique perspective to his subjects be it architecture and interiors or portraits and lifestyle images. He's been featured in many publications and exhibitions but his team will tell you his true passion is beating everyone at ping pong.

Camille Segura

Producer

Camille has 8 years experience in the film industry in France where she worked with talent agents, sold arthouse films and then co-financed about twenty films a year. She finds embracing change is often the way to go and that is how she settled in Dubai with her family where she developed her love for analog photography. She is now overseeing the studio’s activities and materializing projects in the permanent aim to build strong relationships between happy clients and thriving artists.

Zeina Dahlan

Senior Producer

Zeina is of Palestinian-Jordanian origins and has been in the film industry for around 6 years. With a background in Film and television, a Sustainable Development masters, producing is the path she's naturally taken. Producing in multiple countries and dealing with international crew over the years, she has embraced the crazy set life and is always looking forward to creating innovative digital content for clients. From writing the idea to end credits and subtitling, Zeina will enthusiastically lead the project from A-Z

Niño Consorte

Founder
& Photographer

Nino's love for photography started 7 years ago when he began working with Mohamed Somji as at Gulf Photo Plus. He has since become an expert assistant that the whole team relies on and shoots photo and video. His love of the craft has led him to his passion of street photography where his photos capture stolen moments of people and places that tell their stories. On and off set Nino always brings a great attitude and keeps everyone on the team laughing.

Bala Ochangco

Videographer

Bala comes from a family in film production who inspired him to pursue his craft 9 years ago. Keeping creatively at the edge of the cliff, he has won silver at the QS-APPLE Creative Awards and grand prize at the Vision Petron Art Competition. Even with his experience and success his enthusiasm to keep learning and improve his visual storytelling is never ending and inspires his teammates to do the same. In his own time he enjoys cooking and even produces his own cooking show.

Ismail Noor

Photographer

Ismail has a background in advertising where his curiosity brought him behind the camera 5 years ago. He is now one of the best art & culture photographers in the region, shooting regularly for the Louvre Abu Dhabi, Alserkal Avenue and many other galleries. He also specializes in architecture and interior photography and enjoys documenting the streets and daily activities of people in his travels. He is so easy to work with which is a reflection of his many repeat clients.

Augustine Paredes

Photographer

Augustine Paredes is an artist and photographer with a Bachelor's Degree in Mass Communication, and a background in advertising giving him a unique perspective working with clients. His work has been exhibited around the world most recently at Art Dubai and Art Fair Philippines. He is a true creative and brings innovation and imagination to the visual narratives he tells inspired from his many-storied travels and experiences. He is multi-faceted and elevates ST with his work across our platforms.

John Gatapia

Videographer

John is a video creator with almost 6 years of experience. He can handle a broad range of content creation but he especially loves docu-commercial projects. On set he is focused and keeps things simple and the videos he makes continually exceed client expectations. Color grading is his favorite part of the whole process as that seemingly subtle detail can set the mood for the whole story. At the studio he is constantly giving Mohamed Somji a run for his money on the ping pong table.
